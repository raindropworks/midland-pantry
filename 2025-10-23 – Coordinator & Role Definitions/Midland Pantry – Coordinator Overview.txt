Coordinator Overview

Pantry Planning Project

SUMMARY
--------
This document provides an overview of the coordinator team structure for the
Pantry Planning Project, detailing the distinct and shared responsibilities of
each role within the Midland Pantry & Community Food Forest initiative.¹

COORDINATOR STRUCTURE
----------------------
Off-Site Systems & Compliance Coordinator – Heather-Lynne Van Wilde  
  • Oversees documentation, recordkeeping, and grant compliance.  
  • Maintains all digital archives and project version control.  
  • Leads remote coordination, accessibility, and public reporting.  

On-Site Garden & Mutual-Aid Coordinator – Jaide Crow  
  • Manages on-site operations, volunteers, and planting activities.  
  • Develops habitat plans and handles certification documentation.  
  • Coordinates with Indigenous advisors for ecological stewardship.  

Property Manager & Advisor – Anna Dunlap  
  • Represents property management and administrative compliance.  
  • Ensures safety, facility maintenance, and access coordination.  
  • Acts as liaison between the project and Midland Commons management.  

COMMUNICATION PROTOCOL
-----------------------
• Regular team updates occur biweekly or as needed via email or meeting.  
• Major revisions to documentation must be logged before implementation.  
• Coordinators share equal decision-making authority within their domains.
